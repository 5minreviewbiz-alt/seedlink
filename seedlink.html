How it works
Simple as a text message
Three steps between a recommendation and money in your account.
01
📤

Send the referral

A customer asks if you know a good roofer. Open SeedLink, pick the business, enter the customer's phone number. They get a text in seconds.

02
🎟️

Customer claims a discount

The customer taps the link, enters their details, and gets a one-time discount code. The receiving business is notified instantly, then the customer calls and books.

03
💰

You get paid

The job closes and the business reports the sale. Your commission is calculated automatically and paid out. No invoices, no chasing, no awkward conversations.
